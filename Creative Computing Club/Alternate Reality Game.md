# Introduction
Alternate Reality Games are games that involve real world elements that players need to interact to solve one big mystery. ARGs revolve around a common theme and clues are left for the players to find and decode.
# Objective
The objective of this ARG is to engage a collaborative environment and encourage teamwork to solve a mystery. The ARG will span over 3 months for the players to solve, and clues are given at separate timestamps within the 3 months as each clue is revealed.
