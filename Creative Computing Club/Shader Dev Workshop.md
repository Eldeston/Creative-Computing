# Introduction to Shader Dev
Before computers were invented, the art process is made through layers of paint that take a lot of time and effort. In this age, computers can make art from years to seconds in real time through the GPU.

Shaders are powerful programs used to communicate with the GPU.
# Learning Outcomes
- Exercising the C++ language
- Understanding the GPU workflow
- Learning more about linear algebra
- Learning how to write shaders in shadertoy.com
- Expanding your creativity through mathematics
- Improving your problem solving skills in optimization and design
