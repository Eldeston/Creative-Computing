# FAAP Framework
## Function
This is all about what the app can do:
- Touch gestures respond correctly
- App loads quickly
- Instructions or help available
- No crashes or errors
## Aesthetic
This is the design side: 
- Consistency across screens
- Appropriate use of white space
- Graphics support content, not distract
- Animations enhance experience, not annoy 
## Architecture
This is the structure of the app:
- Number of screens
- Navigation menu clarity
- Information hierarchy
## Purpose
This is the why behind the app:
- Who is it for?
- What problem does it solve?
- Why should anyone download it?
- Does it make life easier, faster, or more fun?
