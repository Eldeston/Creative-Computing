# Creative Industry Challenges 4
# Branding
## Color palette
Maintaining the current palette choice which is green. More vibrant colors can exhibit playful energy, more neutral colors exhibit corporate and dull.
## Typography
System fonts are formal, third party fonts are fun.
## Logo/icon
Simplified logos can lose identity and become more corporate, but complexity introduces more identity and become more fun.
## Mission
> "To build athletes of character — confident, skilled, and grounded in values that extend beyond the court. We strive to guide young athletes through purposeful training that enhances not just performance but also leadership, discipline, and integrity."
## Vision
> "To become the leading youth sports academy in the UAE, recognized for excellence in training, integrity in leadership, and impact in the community. Through our programs, we aim to empower young athletes to pursue greatness with faith, focus, and perseverance."
## Values
* Faith in Action: Guided by the principles of our Lord Jesus Christ.
* Discipline: Building focus, respect, and resilience.
* Teamwork: Success comes from unity — on and off the court.
* Excellence: We aim to raise the bar in everything we do.
