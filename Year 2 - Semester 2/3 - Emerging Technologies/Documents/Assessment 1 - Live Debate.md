# Debate Outline
## Introduction (Motion) 1-2 Minutes
Artificial intelligence (AI) in this day and age is incorporated to our daily aspects of our lives ever since its trend in 2022. AI has made many aspects of our lives easier. It is used in many aspects of our lives from everyday tasks, work, art, and so on.

But where do we draw the line when AI threatens to destroy our very identity as humans? What is humanity's stance on its ethical use? Today, we will find out how **artificial intelligence threatens to take over too many aspects of our lives that should be left to humans**, and how we as humans stay informed and be responsible for it.

## Debate Conclusion (Resolution) 1-2 Minutes
Artificial intelligence is a tool that neither has good or ill intent for humanity, but it is up to us how we as humans can stay informed and be responsible on how we will use AI. AI is a double edged sword that can either improve our everyday lives or destroy our identity as humans.
